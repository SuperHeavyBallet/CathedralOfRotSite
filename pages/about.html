The Cathedral of Rot (CoR) is an ever expanding universe I am building as a passion project.

Ever since the mid 90's, I have been enthralled by the dark, gritty, gothic and often hillariously horrible worlds that human minds had conjured, whether that came in video game lore, fiction, films, table top games or really any form of idea manifestation.
